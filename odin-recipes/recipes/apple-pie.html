Apple Pie

Description

This is absolutely the best homemade apple pie you'll ever make! It has a flaky, buttery crust and a tender, lightly-spiced apple pie filling. Use a combination of apples for best flavor, and bake until the top is golden and the filling is bubbly!

Ingredients

  • apples
  • vanilla
  • spices
  • brandy
  • cinnamon
  • honey

How to make

  1. To make it easier to roll out the dough, remove the dough disks from the refrigerator 10 to 15 minutes before using.

  2. Always place a baking sheet under the pie to catch any juices that bubble over so they don't land on the bottom of your oven.

  3. The apple pie is not done until the juices are noticeably bubbling. If you have an instant read thermometer, insert it into the center of the pie. A reading of 200Â°F is done.

  4. If the top of the pie begins to brown too much before it's fully cooked, tent it with foil.
